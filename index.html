Wrangler National Finals Rodeo

The Wrangler NFR is the richest and most prestigious rodeo in the world and an event like no other, an extravaganza for fans to enjoy the roping and riding activity of today’s cowboys.

Events

  • Saddle Bronc

    A rodeo event where a rider uses a specialized saddle to stay on a bucking horse.

  • Barrel racing

    A fast-paced rodeo event where riders navigate a cloverleaf pattern around barrels.

  • Tie down roping

    A rodeo event where a cowboy on horseback ropes a calf and quickly dismounts to tie three of its legs together.

  • Team roping

    Two riders, a header and a heeler, work together to rope a steer in a timed event.

  • Bull Riding

    Riders attempt to stay on a bucking bull for a set time, showcasing strength and skill.
